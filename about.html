Centre for Intelligent Solutions

The Centre for Intelligent Solutions at IISER Pune gathers more than twenty researchers working across data, algorithms, models, applied mathematics, computation, and digital transformation. Their collective mission is to translate frontier science into deployable systems that help industry navigate the disruptions promised by AI, data science, and quantum technologies.

Through joint research, consultancy, student engagements, and placements, the centre forges durable academia–industry partnerships oriented toward social good. Every collaboration is designed to move swiftly from scoping to pilot-ready outcomes, ensuring breakthroughs make a practical difference.

Vision and mission

Guiding principles that shape CIS collaborations and sustain long-term impact.

Vision

  • Provide a focused platform that sustains cooperation between industry and academia in data and analytics.
  • Employ mathematical, simulation, and data science tools to solve problems relevant to industry.

Mission

  • Focus quantitative research on problems of immediate practical interest to industry.
  • Identify emerging trends and ideas ready for rapid deployment.
  • Maintain sustained, mutually beneficial collaborations with industry partners.
  • Boost research and teaching in applied mathematics, data science, machine learning, high-performance computing, and allied areas.
  • Promote student pathways into high-impact quantitative careers.
  • Enable joint academia–industry activities that scale innovation.

Need for the Centre

CIS helps organisations convert unprecedented data growth into actionable intelligence, delivering social value alongside productivity gains.

Industry 4.0 links physical and digital operations, making productivity gains dependent on sophisticated mathematics, data, and AI-driven decision systems. Organisations now generate unprecedented amounts of data and require trusted scientific partners to convert it into actionable insight.

IISER Pune has rapidly grown into a premier science institute in India, with faculty and students recognised nationally and internationally. By launching CIS, the institute combines its expertise in applied mathematics, analytics, data sciences, computing, and simulation to address industry challenges with immediate relevance.

The centre serves as a collaborative platform where academia and industry—and even industry peers—can co-create solutions that accelerate digital transformation while delivering measurable social value.
