Centre for Intelligent Solutions

The Centre for Intelligent Solutions at IISER Pune gathers more than twenty researchers working across data, algorithms, models, applied mathematics, computation, and digital transformation. Their collective mission is to translate frontier science into deployable systems that help industry navigate the disruptions promised by AI, data science, and quantum technologies.

Through joint research, consultancy, student engagements, and placements, the centre forges durable academia–industry partnerships oriented toward social good. Every collaboration is designed to move swiftly from scoping to pilot-ready outcomes, ensuring breakthroughs make a practical difference.

About IISER Pune

A premier institute that blends frontier research with transformative education in the basic sciences.

The Indian Institute of Science Education and Research (IISER) Pune was established in 2006 by the Ministry of Human Resource Development (now the Ministry of Education) to advance research and teaching in the basic sciences. In 2012, it was declared an Institute of National Importance by an Act of Parliament.

As a unique initiative in Indian science education, IISER Pune strives to be a science university of the highest calibre, integrating teaching and research while nurturing curiosity and creativity. The institute is committed to integrity, fairness, dignity, professionalism, equal opportunity, and environmental responsibility.

Research at IISER Pune spans the departments of Biology, Chemistry, Data Science, Earth and Climate Science, Humanities and Social Sciences, Mathematics, Physics, and Science Education. Interdisciplinary collaborations, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant academic community attract experts from India and around the world.
