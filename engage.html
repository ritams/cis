LET'S COLLABORATE

Pick the partnership format that suits your pace.

Partner with CIS to turn challenges into breakthroughs. Mobilize our 19 researchers across 11 domains for workshops to lasting alliances.

Modality of Collaboration

Join individuals and organizations partnering with CIS through customized collaboration modes.

Infrastructure & Corpus Support

Support through corpus creation for setting up infrastructure, research facilities, and human resources required for the Centre’s operations. Naming opportunities are available under this initiative.

Activity & Project Grants

Activity or project based collaborations supporting research, consultancy, training, and outreach through long-term as well as annual grants.

Benefits for all stakeholders

Partnering with CIS connects you to an interdisciplinary hub at IISER Pune.

Benefits for Industry

  • Access to expertise and facilities at IISER Pune—faculty, post-doctoral fellows, students, research facilities, and events.
  • Gain access to human resources with relevant expertise, including student and postdoctoral talent aligned with the Centre.
  • Engage with academia to research and implement new ideas in your systems and processes.

Benefits for Academia

  • The IISER student community benefits from exposure to regular interactions with industry-based practitioners.
  • IISER’s research community discovers new applications for their work.
