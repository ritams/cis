COLLABORATE WITH US

Pick the partnership format that suits your pace.

From discovery workshops to multi-year alliances, CIS aligns IISER Pune researchers with your roadmap, activating 19 researchers across 11 domain tracks.

Modality of Collaboration

Individuals and industry can become partners in this Centre through the following modes.

Infrastructure & Corpus Support

Support through corpus creation for setting up infrastructure, research facilities, and human resources required for the Centre’s operations. Naming opportunities are available under this initiative.

Activity & Project Grants

Activity or project based collaborations supporting research, consultancy, training, and outreach through long-term as well as annual grants.

Benefits for all stakeholders

Partnering with CIS connects you to an interdisciplinary hub at IISER Pune.

  1. Industry benefits from expertise and facilities at IISER Pune—faculty, post-doctoral fellows and students, research facilities, and events.
  2. Industry gains access to human resources with relevant expertise, including student and postdoctoral talent aligned with the Centre.
  3. Industry can engage with academia to research and implement new ideas in their systems and processes.
  4. The IISER student community benefits from exposure to regular interactions with industry-based practitioners.
  5. IISER’s research community discovers new applications for their work.

Want to interact with us?

If you have more questions, want to discuss new possibilities or proposals, or would like to initiate an activity under the aegis of the Centre for Intelligent Solutions, write to cis@iiserpune.ac.in or reach out to a coordinator below.
